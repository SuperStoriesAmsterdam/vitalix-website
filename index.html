"A cardiologist looks at your heart. An endocrinologist looks at your hormones. Your GP has 10 minutes. Nobody looks at you as a system. Vitalix does exactly that."

Most health tools measure one thing. Your fitness tracker counts steps. Your blood test checks twelve values once a year. Your doctor sees you when something is wrong.

But your body is a system. Your heart rate variability tells you something about your nervous system. Your sleep architecture affects your hormones. Your inflammation markers connect to your recovery and your mood.

Vitalix is built on a single premise: the most useful health information comes from connecting the signals, not collecting them in isolation. We bring your continuous wearable data, your periodic lab results, and your genetic predispositions into one coherent picture — updated daily.

No diagnoses. No treatment advice. Just data, organised into understanding.

How it works

Three steps to knowing your system

No complicated setup. No specialist required. You connect the dots — we build the picture.

1
Connect your hardware

Link your Polar Loop and Withings BPM Vision. Add your BBT thermometer readings manually. Vitalix starts receiving your biometric stream immediately — heart rate, HRV, blood pressure, sleep, temperature, and more.

2
Build your baseline

Over the first 4–8 weeks, Vitalix establishes what's normal for you specifically. Not a population average — your rhythms, your patterns, your variance. Upload lab results and DNA data to deepen the picture further.

3
Read your system

Your dashboard shows how the signals interact. When something is shifting, you see it in context — not as an isolated alert, but as part of the broader story your body is telling. Readable by you. Shareable with any doctor.

Built on one idea: coherence is intelligence.

Modern medicine is extraordinary at fixing problems. It is significantly less equipped to prevent them. The tools exist — wearables, lab tests, genomics — but they were never designed to talk to each other. Vitalix is the layer that makes them coherent.

We built Vitalix around a concept we call the health data commons: the idea that the most valuable insights come not from any single measurement, but from the relationships between measurements over time. When your HRV drops the week your inflammation markers rise, that's not two data points — that's a signal. Vitalix is built to see those signals.

Vitalix is a product of SuperStories BV, an Amsterdam-based company building tools that give people meaningful access to their own data. We are not a wellness app. We are not a diagnostics company. We are an information platform — and we hold ourselves to the standards that implies: accuracy, privacy, and honesty about what data can and cannot tell you.

Privacy

Your data. Full stop.

"Your health data is yours. We process it to serve you. We do not sell it, share it, or monetise it in any form. Full stop."

Vitalix is built and hosted entirely within the European Union, under GDPR. Your data is stored encrypted, processed locally where possible, and never used to train models that leave your account. We do not have an advertising business. We do not have data partnerships. Our business model is a subscription — which means our interests are aligned with yours: making the platform genuinely useful, not maximising data extraction.
