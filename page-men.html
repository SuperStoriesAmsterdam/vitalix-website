Symptoms without context

Fatigue, reduced recovery, lower motivation, mild weight gain — these are often dismissed as stress or ageing. When they're measurable physiological signals, they're the earliest warning the body issues. Vitalix makes the invisible visible.

The annual check-up gap

A once-a-year blood test is a photograph. Your cardiovascular system, hormonal profile, and metabolic function change continuously. Twelve data points per year is not monitoring — it's sampling. Vitalix is continuous.

Training harder isn't always the answer

Many men optimise the output — training volume, performance — without understanding the system underneath. HRV, recovery, sleep quality, and hormonal status determine how much your body can adapt. Knowing your floor means you stop ignoring it.
